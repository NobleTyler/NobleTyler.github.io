**SwipOrBut:** This project was used in a research study on the usability of buttons versus swiping.
The project itself gave users a stack of cards they they could button press or swipe through with obvious choices i.e liking icecream disliking spiders

### What I learned from this project
* Proper research protocol. Everything from research proposals to testing users. To signing consent forms.
* Devloping and designing my own new features. These were later tested as a part of my research. 
* Presentation skills, in order to present my research infront of other proffesionals.
