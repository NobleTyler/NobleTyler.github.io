**Fresh Books:** This was a website with a complete front-end and backend. It was done in a group
setting and used HTML/CSS/JS/AJAX on the front-end and Java/ApacheTomcat/SQL on the backend. 

### What I learned from this project
* Group dynamics played a large portion of getting this site done.
* Doing both front and back-end as well as UX  was a rewarding experience
* Working with queries and outputting AJAX proved to be a challenge
